Eidetus is a strategic, team-based BYOH (Build-Your-Own-Hero) shooter set in a dystopian sci-fi landscape. While your psypher channels memories into psychic power, draw from the deck of abilities you’ve assembled to defeat your opponents.

B.Y.O.H.

(Build Your Own Hero)

Reveries

Deck out your reverie to suit any play style, becoming fierce and formidable, quick and nimble, or tactical and disruptive. Each reverie consists of thirty skills and three passives, providing a canvas for a variety of skill combinations.

Gear

Each psypher can be outfitted with an assortment of items. With memories becoming fluid and fleeting, many cosmetics are odd items repurposed as wearable equipment.

How to Play

Equip Skills

Prior to joining a match, you will have to select one of your reveries. This selection determines which skills will be available to you for the entire match. Upon loading in, you will then be randomly given four skills from your reverie which can be reshuffled for a potentially better starting set.

As you play, additional skills will become available. These can be used to overwrite a currently equipped skill to adapt your play style for synergizing with your allies or to better combat the opposing team.

Harvest Resources

Throughout the map, remnants of fallen psyphers can be found in the space between each WOMP landing site. In addition, as players inevitably fall against opposing forces, one of these remnants will coalesce. Siphon the stabilizing energy to boost your maximum mana.

A fusion of ancient technology and volatile energy can be found opposite from the first WOMP. Beware, this relic has drawn the attention of the local creatures and more simple-minded denizens. They must be removed from the area before you can claim the valuable restorative effects.

Claim Victory

Each match contains three WOMPs, powerful founts of energy that any psypher can tap into. Each WOMP a team captures guarantees that one-third of the final WOMP will be automatically secured for them.

The first will land at a neutral location, providing equal opportunity for both teams. The team that manages to harvest this WOMP will be that much closer to victory. Fret not if you fail to capture the first, as the second WOMP will land closer to the currently losing team. This gives a chance to balance the odds before the third and final WOMP spawns in the center of the map.

When a team harvests the third and final WOMP, the match is over and they are victorious.

Unlockables

Skills and Passives

At each level you earn shards, which can be spent on packs of skills. These packs contain a variety of different skills and passives, with varying levels of prestige and power. Every few levels a bonus pack is earned as well, completely on us!

Cosmetics

These wearable accessories are available to deck out your psyphers. Get the most bang-for-your-shard as you mix and match with various outfits.
