How to Play

Equip Skills

Prior to joining a match, you will have to select one of your reveries. This selection determines which skills will be available to you for the entire match. Upon loading in, you will then be randomly given four skills from your reverie which can be reshuffled for a potentially better starting set.

As you play, additional skills will become available. These can be used to overwrite a currently equipped skill to adapt your play style for synergizing with your allies' skills or to better combat those of the opposing team.

Capture Objectives

Each match contains three WOMPs, powerful founts of energy that any esper can tap into. The first will land at a neutral location, providing equal opportunity for both teams. The team that manages to harvest this WOMP will be that much closer to victory.

Drawn to those that narrowly missed the first, the second WOMP will land closer to the currently losing team. This gives a chance to balance the odds before the third and final WOMP spawns in the center of the map. Each WOMP a team captured before the final, guarantees that one-third of the final WOMP will be automatically secured for them.

Throughout the map, additional resources can be claimed on your way to the next conflict. These remnants of fallen espers can be found in the space between each WOMP landing site. In addition, as players inevitably fall against opposing forces, one of these remnants will coalesce. Siphon the stabilizing energy to boost your maximum mana.

A fusion of ancient technology and volatile energy can be found opposite from the first WOMP. Beware, this relic has drawn the attention of the local creatures and more simple-minded denizens. They must be removed from the area before you can claim the valuable restorative effects.

Claim Victory

When a team harvests the third and final WOMP, the match is over and they are victorious.

Gameplay Tips

  • Understand and utilize the game map and enemy movements to your advantage.
  • Composition and cooperation are essential for a successful team. Support each other to effectively implement a strategy for controlling the WOMP and claiming vital resources.
  • Devise inventive combinations of skills in a reverie that dovetail effectively in combat.
  • Design a character that suits your play style, vision and team's needs.

Unlockables

Skills and Passives

At each level you earn shards, which can be spent on packs of skills. These packs contain a variety of different skills and passives, with varying levels of prestige and power. Every few levels a bonus pack is earned as well, completely on us.

Equipment

The cosmetic accessories available to deck out your characters can be unlocked with shards. Each cosmetic can be used on all characters, giving the most bang-for-your-shard while you mix and match with various outfits.
